Embedding RSS Feeds

To embed a RSS feed, surround your feed URL with <rss-feed>

For example:
<rss-feed>https://www.example.com/my-rss-feed</rss-feed>

Embedding JW Videos

To embed a youtube video, surround your URL with <cde-youtube-video>

For example:
<cde-youtube-video>https://www.example.com/my-cde-video</cde-youtube-video>
